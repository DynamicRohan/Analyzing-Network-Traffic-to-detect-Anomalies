It's a dashboard made to make students understand how statistical methods are used to detect anamolies in servers. 
It has used Hypothesis testing and Z-score methods(one-tailed) to detect abnormalities on websites by comparing it to normal behaviour of website.
It shows how statistics is engaging and useful in this modern era to do anything virtually.
It has simulations to make students feel engaging and get interested towards mathematics for better rather than just being bored.
It also has comparison of Z-scores with other methods used to tell how accurate and efficient it is compared to others.
And in which case it is useful and in which it is not.
It shows the methods of taking data and processing it to detect attacks.
It also shows the limitations of Z-score method.
It is only for students to understand statistics better and for teachers to show them and make statistics more interesting.
